About

Fuel The Flame That Illuminates Windsor/Essex

What is Chabad of Windsor?

It is events - like our community holiday events, Students events, which gather hundreds of people - and small events, like Torah classes and one on one counseling. It is high profile - like our public menorah lightings, covered in the media and attended by Jews and non-Jews, and low profile - like the work we do that you will never read about. It is building relationships with other leaders in our community and advocating for our community, and it is reaching out to every single Jew, regardless of their level of observance, socioeconomic status, or anything else. It is ALL about shining a powerful light amidst some awful darkness.

How does a Matching Campaign work?

Running Chabad of Windsor requires a major investment of time, energy, and capital. While our Rabbi and Rebbetzin work tirelessly to ensure that no Jew is left behind, the day-to-day expenses of operating a Chabad Centre can be daunting. Rather than charging membership fees or exorbitant event prices, which could alienate some people, we rely on the generosity of the community that we serve. To that end, a few generous donors have come forward with a proposal to help Chabad thrive this year. They will collectively donate $60,000 to our centre, provided that it is matched by an equal donation from you, our community. For the next 36 hours only, your dollar will have TWO TIMES times the impact!

How can I help?

You can be a partner in everything we do to benefit the residents and students of Widnsor by contributing to our campaign, and by spreading the word to anyone else who would be interested in supporting this community. Maimonides says that every good deed, no matter how small, tips the scale of humanity toward goodness. Today you have the opportunity to see the impact of that deed multiplied many times over. Join us by making a donation today!
